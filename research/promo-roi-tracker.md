# Promo ROI Tracker

## How to Use
1. Before buying a shoutout: Record the model's current sub count
2. Buy the shoutout, log the details
3. After 24-48 hours: Record new sub count
4. Track revenue from those new subs over 7 days
5. Calculate ROI

## Calculations

**Cost Per Sub** = Promo Price รท New Subs
- Good: < $1
- Okay: $1-3
- Bad: > $3

**ROI** = ((7-Day Revenue - Promo Cost) รท Promo Cost) ร 100
- Break even: 0%
- Good: 100%+ (doubled your money)
- Great: 300%+ (4x return)
